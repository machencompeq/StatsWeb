三、完整解答 (Model Solution)

  1. State: Hypotheses and Parameters

Goal: We want to determine if there is a significant relationship (dependence) between customer segment and advertising channel preference at the α = 0.05 significance level.

Hypotheses:

• H₀: Customer segment and advertising channel are independent (there is no relationship between them).

• Hₐ: Customer segment and advertising channel are dependent (there is a significant relationship between them).

• Significance Level: α = 0.05.

  1. Plan: Procedure and Conditions

Procedure: Chi-square Test for Independence.

To perform this test validly, we must verify:

• Random: We assume the 190 customers represent a random sample from the target population.

• Independence: The total sample size n = 190 is less than 10% of all customers.

• Large Counts: All expected counts must be ≥ 5.

○ Expected Count (E) = (Row Total × Column Total) / Grand Total

○ Min Expected: (80 × 90) / 190 = 37.9

○ Since 37.9 ≥ 5, the condition is satisfied.

  1. Do: Calculations

Data Summary:

• Observed (O): Millennial/Social=72, Millennial/Email=38, GenX/Social=28, GenX/Email=52.

• Expected (E): Millennial/Social=57.9, Millennial/Email=52.1, GenX/Social=42.1, GenX/Email=37.9.

Calculation:

• Test Statistic (χ²):

○ χ² = Σ (Oᵢ − Eᵢ)² / Eᵢ

○ χ² = (72−57.9)²/57.9 + (38−52.1)²/52.1 + (28−42.1)²/42.1 + (52−37.9)²/37.9

○ χ² = 3.432 + 3.817 + 4.723 + 5.254 = 17.226

• Degrees of Freedom (df): df = (rows−1)(cols−1) = (2−1)(2−1) = 1.

Two equivalent decision approaches may be used:

Approach 1 — Critical Value Method:

○ Critical Value: Using the Chi-square table for df = 1, α = 0.05: χ²₀.₀₅ = 3.841.

○ Decision Rule: Reject H₀ if χ²_stat > χ²_critical.

○ Since 17.226 > 3.841 → Reject H₀.

Approach 2 — P-value Method:

○ P-value: For χ² = 17.226 with df = 1, the p-value ≈ 0.000033 (< 0.0001).

○ Decision Rule: Reject H₀ if p-value < α.

○ Since 0.000033 < 0.05 → Reject H₀.

Both approaches lead to the same conclusion.

  1. Conclude: Interpretation

May use either of the two approaches below to state decision:

Approach 1 — Critical Value Method:

Since χ² = 17.226 > χ²_critical = 3.841, the test statistic falls in the rejection region. We Reject H₀ at α = 0.05.

Approach 2 — P-value Method:

Since p-value ≈ 0.000033 < α = 0.05, we Reject H₀ at α = 0.05.

Contextual Conclusion: There is sufficient evidence at the 0.05 level of significance to conclude that there is a significant relationship between customer segment and preferred advertising channel. The marketing manager should tailor advertising strategies differently for Millennials (who prefer social media) and Gen X customers (who prefer email campaigns).
